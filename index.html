About Me

Creating Fashion, Crafting Stories

With over a decade of experience in the fashion industry, I have dedicated my life to creating designs that transcend trends and speak to the soul. My work is a celebration of individuality, elegance, and the timeless beauty of well-crafted garments.

Educated at Parsons School of Design and having collaborated with prestigious fashion houses in Paris and Milan, I bring a unique perspective that blends classical technique with contemporary innovation. Each piece I create tells a story of craftsmanship, passion, and artistic vision.
